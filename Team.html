Our Story

    WingMan was born form the idea that technology should make life easier and not more complicated. As a diverse team of engineers, designers, and innovators we came together with a shared passion for AI and robotics and a mission to make a smart drone companion that assit people in their daily lives.

    From our first brainstorming sessions to building our first prototype every step has been driven by our determination to make a meaningful impact on our daily lives. We believe technology should support you by offering real-time help without stress or distraction.

    Guided by our core values of creativity, honesty, and fun, we are building Wingman to be more than just a product. It is a new way to interact with technology that empowers you to live better and more connected.

    Together, we are excited to bring this vision to life and make the power of AI and robotics accessible to all.
